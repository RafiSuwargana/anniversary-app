# Anniversary Videos

This folder contains video files for the anniversary website.

## Opening Video (Required):
Place your anniversary video here with the exact filename:
- **anniversary-video.mp4** (Main opening video that plays after countdown)

## How to Add Your Video:
1. Place your video file in this folder: `public/videos/`
2. Rename your video to: `anniversary-video.mp4`
3. The video will automatically appear after the countdown page

## Video Guidelines:
- **Format**: MP4 (recommended for best browser compatibility)
- **Alternative formats**: .webm, .ogg (optional)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or 1280x720 recommended
- **Duration**: Any length (will have play controls)
- **Size**: Try to keep under 50MB for faster loading
